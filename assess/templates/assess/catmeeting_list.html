Cloud Assessment Team (CAT) Meetings


The cloud assessment team regularly meets to discuss the applications they have recently assessed. The purpose is to determine as a group whether an application is suitable for use within Health. The process to create a CAT meeting is to first create the meeting, then add the applications for discussion (the agenda) and lastly decide whether to whiltelist or blacklist applications.
